Soil Life - About Us

This isn't about going off the grid or back to the land.
You dont have to quit your day job or become a farmer even.
Its just about empowering yourself by growing some of your own food(cultivate a garden)
composting your own shit(crap?), literally and figuratively, and once in a while,
channeling your inner child, kicking off your own shoes and running around barefoot on the soil
from which you come, of which you are made, and to which, one day, you will ultimately return.


Soil Life is a non-profit based out of UC Davis,
supported by the Soils and Biogeochemistry
Graduate Group.

We are on a mission to change the way
the world looks at soil!
